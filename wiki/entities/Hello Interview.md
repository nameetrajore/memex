## Summary

Hello Interview is an interview preparation platform founded by FAANG hiring managers and staff engineers. Their flagship course, "System Design in a Hurry," is used by tens of thousands of software engineers preparing for system design interviews at top tech companies. Based in Seattle, WA.

## Key Offerings

- **System Design in a Hurry** — dense, practical course working backwards from what interviewers actually assess. Covers core concepts, key technologies, patterns, delivery framework, and 28+ practice problems with guided solutions.
- **Low-Level Design in a Hurry** — object-oriented / class-structure design guide.
- **ML System Design in a Hurry** — applied ML engineering interview guide.
- **Guided Practice** — step-by-step interview simulation with AI feedback.
- **AI Tutor** — contextual Q&A grounded in course material.

## Philosophy

They take a "working backwards from the interview" approach — teaching only what actually matters for passing, avoiding ChatGPT-generated filler and unnecessarily deep academic content. Content is kept current with modern hardware capabilities and technologies.
