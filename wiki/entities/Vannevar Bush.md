## Summary

American engineer and science administrator (1890–1974). In his 1945 essay "As We May Think," he proposed the **Memex** — a hypothetical device for storing, linking, and retrieving personal knowledge through associative trails. The Memex vision anticipated hypertext and personal knowledge management. The [[LLM Wiki]] pattern fulfills the part Bush couldn't solve: automated maintenance and cross-referencing.
