## Summary

A preparation roadmap from [[Hello Interview]] for system design interviews. Structured in two phases: building a foundation (understand the interview format, adopt a delivery framework, learn core concepts) and deliberate practice with graded problems across three difficulty tiers.

## Phase 1: Build a Foundation

1. **Understand the format** — Read the [[System Design in a Hurry — Introduction|introduction]] or watch a mock interview.
2. **Choose a delivery framework** — Follow the [[System Design Delivery Framework|Delivery Framework]] to stay structured and avoid scope creep.
3. **Learn the basics** — Study [[Core Concepts for System Design Interviews|Core Concepts]], [[System Design Key Technologies|Key Technologies]], and [[System Design Interview Patterns|Common Patterns]].

## Phase 2: Practice

The recommended loop:
1. Pick a question.
2. Read the requirements.
3. Attempt a solution on your own (whiteboard or guided practice).
4. Compare against the answer key.
5. Run peer mocks with someone at your target company — speaking under time pressure is a different skill from reading.

## Practice Problems

### Easy
Bitly, Dropbox, Local Delivery Service, News Aggregator

### Medium
Ticketmaster, FB News Feed, Tinder, LeetCode, WhatsApp, Yelp, Strava, Rate Limiter, Online Auction, FB Live Comments, FB Post Search, Price Tracking Service

### Hard
Instagram, YouTube Top K, Uber, Robinhood, Google Docs, Distributed Cache, YouTube, Job Scheduler, Web Crawler, Ad Click Aggregator, Payment System, Metrics Monitoring
